Snare Essentials
Master the Marching Snare Drum

Snare Essentials is the ultimate companion for mastering marching snare drum exercises, based on Geoff Fry’s renowned course. Designed to help drummers of all levels improve their skills, the app features over 50 meticulously performed exercises, authentic drum sounds, and a dynamic metronome that ensures precision in every practice session. Track your progress, stay motivated, and improve your technique with advanced features optimized for Apple devices. Start your journey with a FREE 7-day full-access trial and take your drumming to the next level!

Rudimental Mastery System
Achieve Total Rudimental Freedom

Master over 600 rudimental variations with the Rudimental Mastery System, based on Geoff Fry’s acclaimed book Freestyle Rudiments: A System for Rudimental Mastery. Whether you're a beginner exploring rudiment families, an advanced player seeking complete freedom, or a teacher expanding your students’ vocabulary, RMS provides the tools to elevate your skills. With a note cursor, customizable metronome, realistic drum sounds, and five dynamic practice modes, RMS makes learning engaging and effective. Progress tracking and iCloud syncing ensure your practice sessions stay on track across all your devices. Dive into RMS and redefine your approach to rudimental drumming.

Rudimentia
Master PAS and Hybrid Rudiments

Refine your rudiments with Rudimentia, the interactive app based on Ryan Alexander Bloom’s book, Encyclopedia Rudimentia. Featuring all 40 PAS rudiments and a growing list of hybrid rudiments, Rudimentia includes five practice modes, authentic sounds, and a dynamic metronome. Enjoy an immersive practice experience with integrated tracking as you unlock your full drumming potential.
